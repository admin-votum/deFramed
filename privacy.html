Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

deFramed ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

What we collect

deFramed collects minimal data to function:

  • Headlines you submit — when you submit a headline for analysis, it is sent to our server and forwarded to five AI models (Claude, Grok, ChatGPT, Gemini, DeepSeek) for evaluation. We do not store submitted headlines beyond the time needed to process and return results.
  • Usage data — basic anonymous analytics such as app opens and feature usage may be collected by Expo (our build platform) and the device operating system. We do not collect personally identifiable information.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information
  • We do not require registration or login
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not display advertisements

Third party AI services

When you submit a headline, it is processed by the following AI services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — anthropic.com/privacy
  • xAI (Grok) — x.ai/privacy
  • OpenAI (ChatGPT) — openai.com/privacy
  • Google (Gemini) — policies.google.com/privacy
  • DeepSeek — deepseek.com/privacy

Headlines submitted are treated as API queries by these services according to their respective policies.

Data retention

deFramed does not maintain a database of user submissions. Headlines submitted for analysis are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers beyond the duration of the API request.

Children's privacy

deFramed is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
